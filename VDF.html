Vodafone Outsourcing Contract

Vodafone, former MOBIFON Outsourcing Contract Management

  • Project Objective: 

Supplying telecom equipments for Mobifon custommers 

  • Project Description: 

Our company signed with Mobifon (now known as Vodafone) an outsourcing contract in which for each new Mobifon client we supplied (rented on fixed rates) the Last mile equipment's (the radio and routing equipment present in the client’s location).

Monitoring the acquisition, installation, un-installation, maintenance, service, etc of these equipment's and the monthly invoicing of the rent services.

The activity reached ~ 80 customers and ~ 3,000 equipment’s in ~ 2,500 locations nationally distributed).

  • Project Duration :

3 Years (2004-2007)

  • Project revenues :

~2.400.000€

  • Implementation Procedure :

Based on Mobifon requirements and procedures, I established an internal procedure for the execution of the contract. The document flows and responsibilities for each involved party and explained them each step.

Together with my three 3 colleagues created in excel, an easy to access tracking system for all the leased equipment's. (equipment/serial number/location/contract). This system was used also for tracking the issued invoices/payments.

I made weekly reports to Management regarding the cash flows of the activity.

  • Implementation Challenges/Problems :

The clients often called us claiming technical problems that were not real.(ex: they claimed that the equipment was not working and actually it was unplugged).

We issued a checklist for each client to fill before calling us.
