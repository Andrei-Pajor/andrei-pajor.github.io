Prefeasibility Study

Coordination of the execution of a Prefeasibility Study by Deloitte for the execution of the “Băile Someșeni” spa resort.

  • Project Objective: 

Execution of a Prefeasibility Study in order to obtain financing from Exim Bank Hungary (19 million Euro project)

  • Project Description: 

In Cluj Napoca Someseni area in 1880 were found thermal wathers and mud with miraculous effects in the process of treating chronic gastrointestinal diseases and serious skin disease such as psoriasisand arthritis. Since then, the wathrs and mud were used by the people in the area and also across Europe.  When a strong  competition emerged in the mid 70’s from the new, modern tourist housing and entertainment facilities of the times, the spa began its slow decay so that by the late 1980’s it was practically derelict and used only by the sick and the few entertainment-seekers left. Eventually, after 1990, with a careless state management and a disastrous tenant, it gradually lost almost all its clientele and business credibility

  • Implementation Duration :

 3 Months 

  • Implementation Steps:

The owners of the land tried for several years to find the best solution for using the curative effects of the water and mud. After discussins with architects from Germany and Hungary and specialists in geology and thermal spaowners, more concepts were drafted. 

The conclusions of the discussions were that in order to establish a final version for the project and to secure a loan from Eximbank Hungary or any other bank, a feasibility study by a trustworthy company is required. That's how Deloitte was contracted.

I supplied Deloitte with all the documents from the architects, geological studies, equipment technical specification and prices, subcontractor execution prices, competitor prices, legal documents, etc and we created different scenarios that were discussed with all the involved parties. By making several adjustments, the final feasibility study was issued.

  • Implementation Challenges/Problems :
The coordination between all the involved parties was a real challenge, especially keeping track of each parties amendments and communicating them to the others.
