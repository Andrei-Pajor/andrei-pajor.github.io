Integrated Information Systems

Implementation of Integrated Information Systems in three County’s: Teleorman (2001), Giurgiu(2003) and
Cluj-Napoca (2004)

  • Project Objective: 

Implementation of integrated information systems - a VPN at the county level between the County Council and all the City Councils of the county, which provide video conferencing and VoIP services. (there were three different projects - in 2001 Teleorman County/ in 2003 Giurgiu County and in 2004 Cluj county)

I will detail the first - Implementation in Teleorman County - the first functional system in Romania. It was declared "Case of success" by ANIAP National Association of Informaticians from the Public Administration: http://www.aniap.ro/Proiecte_succes/CJTeleorman.zip (at the time of implementation, 2001 it was an advanced thing..)

  • Project Description: 

Our company supplied and Installed in 88+1 locations, IT&C (PC, Webcams, Videophones, Videoconferencing , VoIP server) and radio telecom equipment's. The equipment’s were connected to the Mobifon Network (Vodafone). Mobifon supplied data transmission services and Internet access.

  • Implementation Duration :

4 Months / project

  • Budget

 ~400.000€/project

  • Implementation Procedure :

The first step in geting this project was the wining of the public bid for it's execution. We saw the public anouncement, we established a strategy for the execution, we analized our resources, we negociated with the suppliers, we drafted the technical offer and financial one. After participating to the tender and winning we establishied the implementation schedule with the Beneficiary’s HQ representatives. (Implementation Stages: site visit, technical design, equipment installation). The solution implied the execution of  15m Pylons in most locations.

Besides the above steps, my attributions in the project were:

        - coordinatiton of the installation teams (ours and subcontractors);                                                       - sending weekly reports to the beneficiary in order to present the implementation status.           - daily meetings with the head of the installation teams and with the Technical Director                 - negociating the final contractual conditions with the suppliers/subcontractors.                             - monitoring the project’s Cash Flow - invoicing/payments for supplyers/ subcontractors/           beneficiary.

  • Implementation Challenges/Problems :

We encountered situations when there were no grounding for buildings.

We communicated these shortcomings to the beneficiary and found solutions for them (We executed additionally grounding for the buildings).
