ITS infrastructure

Utilities relocation, construction works and ITS infrastructure supply and execution for the Sebes-Turda highway lot 4

  • Project Objective: 

The management of all the activities involved by the contract.

  • Project Description: 

Utilities relocation works: Telecom; Transgaz; gas distribution; sewage; irrigation; water; electrical – Medium, Low, 110kV, 220kV;

Execution of utilities for buildings and parking’s, public lighting, new electrical and telecom connections execution)

ITS (Intelligent Transport Systems) infrastructure supply and execution

  • Implementation Duration :

 12 months ( 2013-2014)

  • Budget :

7.000.000€

  • Implementation Procedure :

We started by estimating budgets for each activity based on site visits made by specialists/each specialty and drafted a project implementation plan.

We started the design activity/each specialty. From the design, we collected quantities of works and materials/objectives. We elaborated RFQ’s/ each specialty.

We sent the RFQ’s to 3 companies/each specialty for offers and compared them. We checked the solvability of the companies and selected one/each specialty to sign the contract with.

Due to the fact that the project was in Sebes, and our company was in Bucharest, and we established the document flow with the beneficiary, we hired a local PM company for on site management (drafting documentations, collecting quality documents, etc). Their activity was supervised by me.

There were weekly status meetings on site and monthly progress reports sent to the Beneficiary.

I kept track of all the cash flows of the project. (subcontractors, credit lines and Beneficiary invoicing and payments)

  • Implementation Challenges/Problems :

The approval of the documentations process was very slow due to the multiple authorities involved.

Each step of the approval process was strictly monitored and presented as it is to the beneficiary.
