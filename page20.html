Data Center and Disaster Recovery

Data Center and Disaster Recovery implementation

  • Project Objective: 

Data Center and Disaster Recovery construction and furnishing, equipment supply and solution implementation.

  • Project Description: 

Trough a public bid, our company gained a contract for the construction of a Data Center and Disaster Recovery.

The contract consisted in the design of the Data Center and Disaster Recovery and the supply of all the equipment’s.

  • Implementation Duration :

 4 months (2011/2012)

  • Project budget:

 4.000.000€

  • Implementation Procedure :

Based on the Beneficiary’s requirements and procedures, I established the plan for the execution of the contract. The document flows and responsibilities for each involved party and explained them each step. (including the suppliers)

I supervised the implementation (based on the plan) and the budget of the project making reports to Management regarding the cash flows of the activity.

  • Implementation Challenges/Problems :

The beneficiary did not had all the equipment's needed for the implementation of the project. (HAVAC).

We installed all the equipment's and waited for the Beneficiary to finalize his part of the project. After that we resumed the final configuration of the systems.
